About Codice Solutions

Codice Solutions exists to take the repetitive, manual work off a business's plate — the spreadsheets, the follow-ups, the inventory checks, the copy-pasting between tools — and replace it with software that simply runs on its own.

We're a small, hands-on team of engineers and operators who believe automation should feel invisible: it should quietly remove friction rather than add another dashboard to check. Our focus spans three disciplines — AI automations, custom app development, and ecommerce operations management — because most growing businesses need all three working together, not in isolation.

Our Motive

We started Codice because we kept seeing the same pattern: capable teams losing hours every week to work a machine could do faster and more reliably. Our motive is simple — build durable, well-engineered systems that give people their time back, and be honest, transparent partners while doing it.

How We Work

  • Direct communication. You work with the people actually building your system, not a rotating account team.
  • Built to last. We favor maintainable, well-documented systems over quick hacks.
  • Outcome-focused. Every engagement starts with the business problem, not the tech stack.

Privacy & Policies Disclaimer

Codice Solutions is committed to handling your information responsibly. Below is a summary of the policies we maintain as a technology agency. This overview is provided for transparency and does not replace a formal legal agreement, which can be furnished on request.

Data Confidentiality

Any information you share with us — project details, business data, credentials, or personal contact information — is kept strictly confidential. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. Access is limited to team members directly working on your project, and data is used solely to deliver the services you've engaged us for.

Mandatory Policies We Maintain

  • Privacy Policy — governs what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how long we retain it, and your rights to access, correct, or request deletion of it.
  • Terms of Service — defines the terms under which our website and services are provided, including acceptable use and liability limits.
  • Data Protection & Security Policy — outlines the technical and organizational safeguards (encryption, access controls, secure storage) used to protect client and user data.
  • Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) — available for client engagements to formally protect proprietary business information shared during a project.
  • Cookie Policy — explains what cookies or tracking technologies this site uses, if any, and how visitors can manage their preferences.
  • Intellectual Property Policy — clarifies ownership of code, designs, and deliverables produced during a project once handed over to the client.
  • Refund & Engagement Policy — sets clear expectations around project scope, payment milestones, and cancellation terms.
  • Incident Response Policy — a defined process for detecting, reporting, and responding to any data security incident promptly.
  • Compliance — alignment with recognized data protection standards and regulations relevant to the regions we operate in (e.g. GDPR-style principles for personal data handling).

Your Assurance

We treat every client relationship as a position of trust. Your data is never used for anything beyond delivering your project, is not shared outside the immediate project team, and can be permanently deleted from our systems upon request once an engagement concludes.
