Governance

Privacy & Data Security Policy

1. Client Operational Data Custody

Codice Solutions enforces complete compartmentalization of all diagnostic audit files. Any telemetry datasets, database API requests, or administrative log files shared during system integration tests are hosted inside isolated Virtual Private Clouds (VPC) protected by AES-256 stateful decryption algorithms. We do not aggregate, sell, or rent your operational telemetry.

2. Source Code, Secrets, & Key Management

Our deployment workflow adheres to zero-trust standards. Production database secrets, proprietary API keys, and environment variables are processed through secure hardware storage modules. Access privileges to testing channels are periodically cleared, and full cryptographic sovereignty is returned to the client upon project launch.

3. Cookie Governance & Digital Footprint Tracking

We collect only the essential details submitted via our project booking forms (Your Name, Email, Budget, Timeline, Service selection) strictly to process and estimate engineering requirements. No tracking cookies or commercial ad pixels are implemented across our digital surfaces.

4. Compliance & Contact Enquiries

For queries regarding server access limits, active compliance certifications, or data purging requests, contact our engineering founders directly at codice.founders@gmail.com.
